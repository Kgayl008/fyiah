Fyiah

Fyiah is an up-and-coming singer/songwriter, born in Mandeville, Jamaica, and raised in Detroit, MI. Influenced by her Reggae roots, her sound also blends elements of Soul, Hip-Hop, and Pop, creating a unique fusion that reflects her journey. Her biggest inspiration is the legendary Bob Marley, whose music and message of unity and love deeply resonate with her.


Fyiah has performed at various venues in Detroit, captivating audiences with her soulful energy. With the release of her debut EP, *Purity*, she is now reaching new audiences and spreading her message through music. Fyiah’s artistry is a testament to her roots and her vision of uniting people through the power of song.
